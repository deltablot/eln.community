Welcome to ELN.community

Share your completed experiments with other laboratories and contribute to open science.

Please note this is a beta version. It is there to gather feedback on the main needs for such an application.
Some features are not yet implemented, but we are working on them.

  • Email notifications are not sent yet for comments and moderation.

Requested features

  • Add a button to report an inappropriate entry.
  • Add a star rating system for entries.
  • Browse templates by type, such as experiment or resource.
